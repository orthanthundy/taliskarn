Pantheon

The Gods—or, as they are also called, the Forebears—of Taliskarn. You can learn more about their role in the world’s history and its peoples in the Myth of Taliskarn.

Work in Progress
This section—and with it, the Myth—is actively being refined and expanded as the details of all regions, peoples, and cultures are developed. Minor divine entities, demigods, and spirits will be added, along with descriptions of cults and beliefs in Malkona. It is possible—and even quite likely—that there will be some changes to the Myth. Only then will I unify this page, bringing it to a consistent format, possibly dividing it into subsections.
However, the core concept and an understanding of the elder gods can already be gathered from here and from the mythology.

Table of Content

Ocean

Children: Kaelesta, Flueré
The primordial deity, still wrathful toward its children and striving to destroy Malkona.

Sun

Children: Solkalor
The primordial deity of the outer world. It serves as an object of worship for some peoples and cultures, though more often, prayers are directed to the Son of the Sun, Solkalor.

Kaelesta

Other names: Mistress of the Winds, Patron of Storms, Wayward One, Mother of Deception
Domains: Storm, Deception
Children: Flammars, Aurehjo, Tranqa
The Lady of the Wind, born from the breath of Ocean, Kelesta presides over the clear sky, storms, and inconstancy. She is revered as the goddess of tempestuous wrath and free-spiritedness, whose deception divided the peoples.

Flueré

Other names: Mother of All Currents, Defiant Daughter, Protector
Domains: Life, Rest
Children: Merfolk (and merrow, respectively), Bryliehns, Kustopha, Fluaht
Born from the trembling of the Ocean, the mother of the first life in Taliskarn. Patron of flowing waters, healing, and the passage of souls.

Solkalor

Other names: Son of the Sun, Scorcher
Domains: Life, Light, Order
Children: Flammars
The child of the Sun who descended to Taliskarn due to his blazing love for Flueré. His light brought life to the lands of Malkona, and his wrath dried and shattered them. Revered as the patron of law, light, and justice.

Ferrudé

Other names: Lord of the Molten Depths, The Fierce One
Domains: Storm, War, Light, Forge
Children: Flammars
Awakened by Flueré, the ruler of volcanoes, fire, and underground forges. The strikes of his mace shaped the mountains, and his union with Kelesta birthed the Scourge of Malkona: the fierce people of the Flammars. Revered as the patron of destructive force, fury, and wild flame.

Aurehjo

Other names: The Father, One-Handed, The Cripple
Domains: War, Order
Children: All humans (Prymars, Arydars, Saltuars, Brunars), Kustopha, Fluaht
A golden-haired giant, deceitfully born of Kelesta by Solkalor, the Father of Humans. Patron of warriors, bravery, and honor.

Tranqa

Other names: The Phantom Moon
Domains: Magic, Peace, Twilight
Children: Noctids
Daughter of Kelesta, born of deception—the Phantom Moon shielding Noctaria from the sun’s light. Revered as the goddess of peace, nocturnal mysteries, and the fragile balance between light and darkness.

Dh’Fhalbh

Other names: The Departed
Domains: Knowledge, Magic, Forge
Children: Kyonnhs
The name given to Solkalor by his last children before the Son of the Sun left Malkona. The god-patron of the Kyonnhs: they believe he still walks among them in the guise of a common craftsman. Patron of knowledge, technology, and free will.

Kustopha

Other names: Goddess of the Source, The Bleeding One
Domains: Nature, Peace, Magic
Followers: Bryliehns
Guardian of the Source, daughter of Flueré and Aurehjo. Her azure blood sustains life in the sacred river Sanguama, and thus, in all of Bryliehn. She presides over harmony, the flow of rivers, and the natural order of things.

Fluaht

Other names: Flumorn, The Alchemist, The Renegade, The Exile
Domains: Knowledge, Death
Children: Neblings (tbd)
The son of Aurehjo and Flueré, who went too far in his care for the Bryliehns. Patron of disease, decay, corruption, and forbidden alchemy, the grim lord of the Fetid Fens.
